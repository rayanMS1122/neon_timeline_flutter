# Advanced UI 5.0

## Visual systems

### Horizon

Airy blue surfaces, large radii, restrained depth, and warm secondary accents.
Designed for modern SaaS and planning products.

### Obsidian

Dense black surfaces with acid-lime focus and violet secondary accents.
Designed for command centers, developer tools, and operations dashboards.

### Paper

Warm editorial surfaces with tight borders and low visual noise.
Designed for portfolios, reports, education, and content-heavy products.

### Signal

Dark or light high-energy surfaces with violet and teal accents.
Designed for product studios and advanced planning workspaces.

## New surfaces

### TimelineFocusView

A strong current-task hero, next-task context, scheduling metrics, and upcoming
flow. It is intentionally calmer than a full calendar.

### TimelineBoardView

Horizontal status or category columns with controller-backed selection and
custom card support.

### TimelineMatrixView

Resource rows against configurable time slots. Entry bars are positioned by
their actual time ranges and remain tappable.

### TimelineOverviewStrip

A compressed three-lane overview rendered with one custom painter. It provides
a fast seek interaction without building a widget for every marker.

### TimelineScenarioCompareView

Added, removed, and modified work is displayed with aggregate metrics and
field-level change categories.

### TimelineCommandPalette

Searchable, keyboard-focused commands for navigation, filters, themes, and
host-defined actions.
