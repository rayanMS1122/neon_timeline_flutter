# Public widgets 12.0

Entry composition:

- `UltimateTimelineEntryCard<T>`
- `UltimateTimelineEntrySurface<T>`
- `UltimateTimelineEntryHeader`, `Body`, `Footer` and `Actions`
- `UltimateTimelineMicroEntry<T>` and `CompactEntry<T>`
- `UltimateTimelineMultiDayEntry<T>`

Interaction composition:

- drag layer, feedback, placeholder, target, guide, conflict and blocked UI;
- auto-scroll indicator and immediate custom drag handle;
- resize layer, preview and large-hitbox handle.

Header composition:

- `UltimateTimelineHeader`
- `UltimateTimelineDateNavigator`
- `UltimateTimelineWeekStrip`
- `UltimateTimelineMetrics`
- `UltimateTimelineViewControls`
- `UltimateTimelineFilterBar`
- `UltimateTimelineResponsiveActions`

All widgets are exported from `timeline_v12.dart` and can be used without
`UltimateStructuredTimeline<T>`.
